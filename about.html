WE ARE:

Broadway Performance club

The Broadway Performance Club was established in 2007 by a group of students who are passionate about musical theatre. Every year a new show is being produced at AUBG. The work starts in the beginning of the school year up until the premiere in early Spring. The weekly schedule includes more than 18 hours of dancing, acting and singing rehearsals. The cast consists of around 30 people from various nationalities with little or extensive experience in the performing arts, but who are all excited to be a part of something special. Each musical is organized entirely by students - the music, directing and choreographing of the show. Students are also the ones finding sponsors, planning out the tour and doing promotion on various platforms. With their nine projects since 2008 they were able to attract more than 40,000 spectators, and their events were covered by some of Bulgaria's biggest media. Working with famous companies that provide copyright for theatrical musicals, they became the first amateur troupe in the world to present "Hairspray" and "Memphis" on stage. They were among the first in Europe to receive the right to stage "Chicago." In 2012 they performed the musical "Grease" in Bulgaria's biggest hall in the National Palace of Culture in Sofia. And ever since 2011 all of Their performances are accompanied by a life orchestra. Thanks to the unique experience that they give to their public, they have created numerous fans not only in Bulgaria but also in Europe and America. In the last three years they were sponsored by the American Embassy in Bulgaria and a numerous big companies such as Ernst and Young, Deloitte, Schwarzkopf, Telerik and many more.
